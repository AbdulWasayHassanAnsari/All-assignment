Muffin Recipe

A basic recipe. you can add preety much anything to it!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 250ml milk
  • 125ml vegetable oil
  • 400gm plain flour
  • 3tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Method

  1. preheat oven to gas mark 6 or a medium temperature.line muffin tray with paper muffin casses.
  2. Beat the eggs in a mixing bowl and then mix in the oil and milk.Add the sugar and whisk until dessolved.
  3. Sift in the dry ingrediants:flour,baking powder and salt and mix until smooth.NB:add any extra ingrediants now eg.chocolate chips/fruit
